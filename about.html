Hi, I'm Jared

Since 2018, I have been designing software products for brilliant organisations. Based in Cape Town, I have worked with Agile teams, both remote and in-person.

Some of the ways I have made an impact include:

  • Using tools such as wireframes, user journeys and prototypes to efficiently and collaboratively design a wide range of products
  • Conducting user research to help companies know what their users really want
  • Identifying the need for, and implementing, design systems to guide companies’ design processes
“Jared is a creative individual with a strong visionary attitude” – Kayleigh, Boost&Co

My work aims to solve problems with elegantly-designed digital products. To achieve this, I follow some core principles:

  • User-centric design: always put the user first
  • Less is more: avoid the temptation to add more features or clutter without being intentional
  • Teamwork: we all have great potential, but what we are capable of increases dramatically when we work together
