Atmocast Weather

The weather, but sexy

Product Designer | UX Researcher | Software Developer

Everything You Need, Nothing You Don’t

Atmocast is a minimalist weather website and mobile app, designed to take the visual and informational clutter out of the act of checking the weather forecast.

Features of Atmocast include:

  • Detailed hourly and daily forecasts
  • Location favouriting
  • Weather-based background colours
  • Available on mobile, tablet and desktop

Want more? Get Pro.

Atmocast Free includes essential weather information for daily use, however Atmocast’s capabilities don’t stop there.

Atmocast Pro offers and extended range of features, for $1/month. These include:

  • More weather details, such as moon phases, dew point and UV index
  • Unlimited favourite locations
