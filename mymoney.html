Get on track with your finances

For the 3 million+ users of the Absa Banking App, MyMoney is a tool that offers the chance to get on top of personal finance.

With spend categorisation, budgeting, portfolio analysis and elements of gamification, MyMoney is brings together a userâ€™s financial information into a succinct and usable product.

Learn, then do

MyMoney aims to assist users in understanding their financial position by providing insights into where their money goes every month, and where their net worth sits. This, however, is only the beginning.

Once a user has begun to learn about how they use their money, MyMoney guides them in the direction of spending less and saving more, through gamification and carefully curated communications.
